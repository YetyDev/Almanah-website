Our Identity

Who We Are

Almanah Care and Love Outreach Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to restoring hope, fostering resilience, and establishing self-reliance for vulnerable widows, young women, and children. We operate on the foundational belief that empowerment transforms families and secures future generations.

Dignity & Support

Protecting and elevating social standing through empathetic care.

Skills Acquisition

Establishing stable pathways to long-term financial self-reliance.

Child Education

Fulfilling basic rights through scholarships and resources.

Community Advocacy

Dismantling barriers and shifting local narratives around widowhood.

Our Core Programs

Creating systemic pathways for development, education, and direct community intervention.

Widow Empowerment Initiative

We provide comprehensive vocational skills programs (including tailoring, catering, farming, and digital literacy) combined with business management education and start-up capital.

  • Vocational workshops
  • Micro-finance grants
  • Business mentorship
Apply or Nominate

Young Women Leadership & Development

Equipping young girls and women in rural communities with mentorship, reproductive health education, career development networks, and foundational leadership tools.

  • Mentorship match-ups
  • Health & hygiene seminars
  • Scholarships for secondary school
Nominate a Leader

Child Welfare & Educational Support

Securing basic education for orphans and vulnerable children. We cover tuition fees, provide textbooks, uniforms, write-in pads, backpacks, and run weekend literacy classes.

  • Tuition sponsorship
  • Learning kits distribution
  • School lunches support
Sponsor a Child

Sustainable Community Outreach

Direct emergency interventions, including free basic healthcare assessments, distribution of essential food packages, warm blankets, and community-wide rights education.

  • Basic medical camps
  • Food & supply distribution
  • Widowhood rights advocacy
