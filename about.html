Who We Are

About Almanah

We are a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to restoring hope, fostering resilience, and establishing self-reliance for vulnerable widows, young women, and children.

Our Mission

To restore hope, foster resilience, and establish self-reliance for vulnerable widows, young women, and children through education, skills acquisition, advocacy, and holistic community-based support programs.

Our Vision

A society where every widow is empowered to lead a dignified, independent life, every young woman is equipped to realize her full leadership potential, and every child has unfettered access to quality education.

Our Journey

A look at the milestones and steps that shaped Almanah Love and Care Foundation.

2021

Foundation & Launch

Almanah Love and Care Foundation was officially registered and established. Our early efforts focused on researching local widow welfare policies and identifying target rural communities needing immediate care and support.

2022

Widow Empowerment Initiative

Launched our flagship skills acquisition workshops. Over 150 widows were trained in micro-farming, tailoring, and catering. We provided graduation packages containing sewing machines, farming inputs, and seed capital.

2023

Child Welfare Expansion

Recognizing the impact of economic hardship on child enrollment, we introduced secondary school educational scholarships and distributed over 500 school relief kits (backpacks, books, writing materials, and uniforms) to orphans.

2025

Advocacy & National Reach

Expanded our outreach program to cover 15+ communities nationwide. We partnered with legal organizations to run education seminars focusing on widowhood inheritance rights and gender equality advocacy.
