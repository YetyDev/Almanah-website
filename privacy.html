Legal & Safety

Privacy Policy

We hold your trust in the highest regard. Our Privacy Policy details our unwavering commitment to protecting your personal information and ensuring data safety.

Secure & Trusted

256-Bit SSL Encryption & secure database storage protocols active.

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Almanah Love and Care Foundation ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, beneficiaries, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you interact with our website at almanahcarefoundation.org and through our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when using our services. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and messages you submit through our contact forms.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Your email address when subscribing to receive updates and reports.
  • Donation Data: Information required to process financial contributions, including donation amount, currency, and name (processed securely through third-party processors).
  • Nomination Information: Personal stories or details you choose to share when nominating a widow, child, or community beneficiary for support.

3. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected information to fulfill our humanitarian mission and operate our services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and coordinate volunteer or partnership efforts.
  • Process donations securely and generate tax-deductible or official donation certificates.
  • Deliver newsletters, impact reports, and updates on our programs.
  • Evaluate beneficiary nominations and follow up on eligibility.
  • Optimize our website performance, layout, and user experience.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed designed to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification. Financial transactions are encrypted and transmitted securely via certified payment providers. We do not store credit card or raw payment details on our servers.

5. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy. Almanah Love and Care Foundation does not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information if required by law, to enforce our site policies, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as Facebook, Instagram, or partner sites). These platforms operate independently and maintain their own privacy policies. We do not accept responsibility or liability for their content, policies, or activities.

7. Your Rights & Choice

You may opt-out of receiving newsletter emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our communications or by contacting us directly. You also have the right to request updates, corrections, or erasure of your personal data stored by us.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at:
